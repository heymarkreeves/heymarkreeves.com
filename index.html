Mark Reeves

Mark Reeves is a software engineer with more than 25 years experience building websites and web applications, working at Discourse.

Mark has supported website & marketing platforms for the Campbell Soup Company, Lexmark, The New York Stock Exchange, Fidelity Investments, ESPN, MINI USA, Cadillac, and SAP.

Prior to joining Discourse, he partnered with Higher Ed teams to design and build web apps for citizen science projects and the digital humanities; built an online insurance estimate calculator, and a customized online ordering system for a high-end catering firm; synchronized websites and CRM platforms via API integrations; and launched a series of CMS websites for clients in the Healthcare and Biosciences industries.

Mark grew up in the Hudson Valley and Thousand Islands regions of New York State. He earned a B.S. in Telecommunications Management, magna cum laude, at Ithaca College, with a minor in Computer Science. He began his career in Boston, working in digital marketing and software development. In 2016 Mark and his family moved to Vermont.
